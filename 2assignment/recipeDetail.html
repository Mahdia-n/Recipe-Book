Kabuli Palou

ingrediants

  • 3 Cup Rice
  • 2 Sliced Onions
  • 1.5 lb Lamb
  • 350 gram Julienned Carrots
  • 1/4 C Seasame oil

Instructions:

  1. Let the rice soak.
  2. Now prepare the lamb in a pressure cooker. Then add oil and heat it.
  3. Then add the lamb to rice and add a tablespoon of salt. Finally, add 3 glass of water and heat it for one hour.
  4. Now cook ypur carrot and raisins.

Puff Pastry

ingrediants

  • 300 gram flour
  • Salt and Water
  • 100 gram Butter

Instructions:

  1. Blend everything together with a pastry cutter or two knives.
  2. Then take a moment to assess the appearance of the dough
  3. Shape everything into a square ship and roll the dough out into a long rectangle. Fold it like a letter.
  4. Put it in refrigerator for one hour.
